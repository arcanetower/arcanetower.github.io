The Council of the Arcane Arts

If you choose magic you will never be able to return to the life you once lived. Your world may be more ... exciting ... but it will also be more dangerous. Less reliable. And once you begin to walk the path of magic, you can never step off of it. - Neil Gaiman

Welcome to the Tower of the Arcane Arts it has had a furtive history, once a beacon of hope for all those looking to study the mystical arts. It spent many years collecting items of great magic labelled Mysteriis Arcanum. However one member of the tower at the time stole some artifacts and set the tower to ruin spreading the wizards out amongst the land. After some time Julius Lionglade decided it was important the tower was renewed and sent the call out for anyone seeking all those with magical ability or wanting to learn to come and take residence in the tower with the hope of restoring it to its former glory.
